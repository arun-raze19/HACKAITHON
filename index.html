About the Hackathon

ORGANIZED BY MAILAM ENGINEERING COLLEGE, HACKAINATION BRINGS TOGETHER THE BRIGHTEST MINDS TO SOLVE REAL-WORLD PROBLEMS USING AI AND MACHINE LEARNING.

Event Schedule

Part 1: Online

APRIL 30, 2025

VIRTUAL PLATFORM

Event Description

The online phase of hackAInation is a 24-hour virtual hackathon where participants will work on AI and ML-based solutions to real-world problems. This phase allows teams to work remotely while being mentored by industry experts.

Rules & Regulations

  • Teams must consist of 2-4 members
  • All team members must be registered
  • Projects must be original work
  • AI/ML components must be significant
  • Code must be submitted before deadline
  • Teams must present their work via video call

Part 2: Offline

MAY 5, 2025

MAILAM ENGINEERING COLLEGE

Event Description

The offline phase of hackAInation is a full-day event at Mailam Engineering College, featuring project presentations, workshops, and networking opportunities. This is where teams will showcase their solutions and compete for prizes.

Rules & Regulations

  • All team members must be present in person
  • Teams must bring their own laptops and equipment
  • Presentations must be within the time limit
  • Dress code: Business casual
  • No external help during presentations
  • Follow all college rules and regulations
